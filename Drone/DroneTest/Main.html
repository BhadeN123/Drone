--------------------Toy Drone-------------------

Read the instructions before playing the game

There are mainly 5 actions you can do in this game...

Until you place the drone , you are not able to perform any other action

  • To place the drone

    Click on any place on the grid to place the drone

         (or)

    Click on place button and enter valid x and y coordinates (0 to 9) also enter the direction of the drone

  • To move the drone

    Press the 'Arrow Up' key on keyboard to move the drone one step ahead in current direction

         (or)

    Click on move button to move the drone one step ahead in current direction

  • To attack

    Attack will fire a projectile two units ahead in the current direction

    Press the "Space bar" key on keyboard to attack

         (or)

    Click on attack button to attack

  • To turn Left or Right

    Press left arrow key to turn left (or) Press right arrow key to turn right

         (or)

    Click on left button to turn left (or) click on right button to turn right

  • To report

    Report will tell the current location of drone

    Press "L" key on keyboard to report

         (or)

    Click on report button to report
